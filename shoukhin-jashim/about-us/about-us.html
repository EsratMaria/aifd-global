EVERY BRAND HAS A STORY TO TELL

"FASHION IS AN IMPORTANT PART OF MY LIFE, I ENJOY CREATING DISTINCTIVE DESIGNS AND LIVING IN THEM, IT IS THE CENTRE OF MY UNIVERSE."
*The Allure of Shoukhin Jashim, Since 2010

Since 2010, Shoukhin Jashim has instilled a creative spirit in Bangladesh, and it is known for its unique, imaginative, and revolutionary visions of ready-to-wear and common elements transformed into luxurious collections. Ms. Shoukhin Jashim envisioned taking her label to the next level while working on that. She decided to pursue her graduate degree at a sustainable fashion institute in India. After successfully running her company in Bangladesh, she is now spreading it Overseas.

Our brand is noted for creating more artistic and innovative things with a hint of good taste. But, as usual, the designer draws on her sophisticated style, with essential components of the designs primarily inspired by vintage essence.
